About Our Organization

Our Vision

To be the leading non-profit early childhood education provider in our community, creating a foundation for lifelong learning and success. We envision a world where every child, regardless of their background, has access to quality early education that nurtures their natural curiosity, creativity, and potential.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, high-quality early childhood education and care services to children from diverse backgrounds, particularly those from underprivileged communities. We are committed to fostering holistic development through play-based learning, nurturing environments, and strong partnerships with families and the community.

Success Stories

Learning Through Play

Our innovative play-based curriculum has transformed how children learn. Little Sarah, who was initially shy and withdrawn, now confidently leads group activities and has developed strong social skills through our structured play programs.

Children engaging in educational play activities

Community Garden Project

Our community garden initiative has not only taught children about healthy eating but also brought families together. The project has grown from a small vegetable patch to a thriving garden that supplies fresh produce to local families.

Children tending to the community garden

Reading Program Success

Our literacy program has achieved remarkable results, with 95% of our graduates entering primary school with above-average reading skills. This success has attracted attention from educational institutions across the region.

Children participating in our reading program

Parent Involvement Program

Our parent involvement initiative has strengthened family bonds and improved learning outcomes. Parents now actively participate in their children's education, creating a supportive learning environment both at school and at home.

Parents and children learning together

Latest News

Dr. Masipha Pre-School Achieves Major Milestones in Early Childhood Education

December 15, 2024

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Dr. Masipha Pre-School is proud to announce several significant achievements that demonstrate our commitment to excellence in early childhood education and our impact on the community.

Key Achievements:

  • 1000+ Children Served: We have successfully provided quality education to over 1,000 children since our establishment, with 95% of our graduates successfully transitioning to primary school.
  • Community Recognition: Received the "Excellence in Early Childhood Education" award from the Department of Education for our innovative curriculum and community outreach programs.
  • Infrastructure Expansion: Completed construction of two new classrooms and a state-of-the-art playground, increasing our capacity to serve 50 additional children.
  • Partnership Success: Established partnerships with three local businesses and two international organizations, securing sustainable funding for our programs.
  • Staff Development: All our teachers have completed advanced training in early childhood development, ensuring the highest quality of education for our children.

These achievements position Dr. Masipha Pre-School as a leading institution in early childhood education and make us an attractive partner for sponsors and donors who want to make a lasting impact on children's lives.
