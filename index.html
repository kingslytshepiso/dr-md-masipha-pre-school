About Our Organization

Our Vision

Inspiring curiosity, independence, and lifelong love for learning. Helping learners become contributing members of the society. In addition, Doctor M.D Masipa day care Centre's vision is to also provide all the children with strong self-esteem, belief in their individual self-awareness, and lastly the ability to express their needs and to stand up for themselves in the future. Lastly we encourage these children to believe and respect their fellow human beings while improving their ability to communicate and collaborate with others.

Our Mission

The mission of the NPO is to provide exceptional care to children while fostering each child's intellectual, social, and physical moral development in a school environment while inspiring the kids to expand their inherit creativity, their desire to learn and their eagerness to explore the world beyond school. Lastly providing the necessary environment that the kids are happy in, safe in, and respected in while they are surrounded by honest and brilliant mentors in the educators.

Success Stories

Empowering Educators

Doctor M.D Masipa Pre-school's full commitment to teacher development has been a success for the last 10 years since the founding of the NPO. Teacher training programs have been implemented which allows teachers to go on school trips, workshops, and conferences to better educate them on how to handle the kids at the school. They also make use of mentorship dynamism which highlights the importance of experienced educators to guide the less experienced educators.

Doctor M.D Masipa Pre-school's dedicated team of educators working together

Inspiring Future Leaders

Doctor M.D Masipa Pre-school encourages leadership skills for their young children at a young age to prepare them for a bright future ahead of them. They have launched teacher leadership programs which explains the pre-school's curriculum and the kind of teaching methods the educators should make use of to make sure the kids understand them. They are provided with alphabets to help them say, name, and write down their names on a daily basis to better grasp them. Learner ship programs are also utilized where the kids are given leadership roles to further prepare them for the future.

Children learning and developing leadership skills in our nurturing classroom environment

Passion for Learning

Doctor M.D Masipa Pre-school educators recognizes the importance of teaching the children about various aspects of life which includes religion. While the school focuses on childhood development, they also strive to encourage diversity, inclusivity, and social-emotional learning. The educator is seeing teaching the children about the story of Solomon. This shows how important it is for these young children to embrace god in their pursuit for knowledge.

Our vibrant learning environment where children develop their passion for knowledge

School Development Underway

The principal of Doctor M.D Masipa Pre-school announced the building of a new school which will provide essential services to the community, including education and social benefits. This will allow the school to move their children out of the donated mobile classes which they got from their sponsors to the new complete school which is being built soon. Furthermore, this will increase publicity of the school and make for new sponsorships and donations which will help increase the organization's reputation.

Current mobile classrooms that will be replaced with the new school building

Latest News

Doctor M.D Masipa Celebrates Big Milestone: Over 5000 Children Graduated Since Founding

August 25, 2025

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Doctor M.D Masipa Pre-School, an esteemed day care Centre in Kgapane Park Town is happy to announce a significant milestone, where over 5000 children have graduated from the pre-school since its founding. This remarkable achievement will be celebrated during the 2025 graduation ceremony where the new batch of students will graduate and join the ranks of Doctor M.D Masipa Pre-school's esteemed graduates or alumni.

There will also be teacher awards for the amazing educators who work all year to ensure that these children are given the best education possible. Parents, and the rest of the community are invited to join us in the celebration Doctor M.D Masipa Pre-school principal Maake Eunice Gugu said. The 2025 graduation will take place on the 10th of December 2025 at Stand No 1415, Park Town, GA-Kgapane, 0838 and all the parents, guardians, and members of the community are invited to join us for this wonderful occasion.
